# TheftOfFire

**Prometheus tricks Zeus, steals fire, and is nailed to a rock for it. The eagle does not stop.**

Story #4 — the era **`theft-of-fire`**, which hands on to **`ages-of-man`**.

```kotlin
compileOnly("net.crewco:mythos-addon-api:0.1.0")
```

## The mechanic worth knowing

**The trick at Mecone opens a two-slot GUI on Zeus's screen.** A glistening pile of fat, an ugly grey
stomach. He takes the shiny one — he is a god, he chooses with his eyes — and under the fat there are only
bones. A myth that turns entirely on one player choosing between two things wants to *be* a two-slot menu.

**The eagle cannot kill him, and that required no code.** The engine already refuses lethal damage to a
Titan from nothing at all, so it takes him to the edge every three minutes and leaves him there.

**`/power endure speak` ends the punishment instantly.** He knows the secret Zeus would trade everything for.
The button exists purely so that *not pressing it* is a decision a player makes, every day, on purpose.
